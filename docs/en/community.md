## Contributing

### Submit Issues

Found a bug or have a new feature suggestion? Please submit an issue on GitHub:
1. Visit [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/eam2539/RustPlanet/issues)
2. Click "New Issue"
3. Choose the appropriate template
4. Describe your issue or suggestion in detail

## FAQ

### Q: Where can I find other developers?
A: Join our Discord server or QQ group, where there is an active developer community.

### Q: How do I report a bug?
A: Submit an issue on GitHub, use the bug report template, and provide as much detail as possible.
